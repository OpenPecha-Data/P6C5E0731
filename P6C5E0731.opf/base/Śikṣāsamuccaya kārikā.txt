śikṣāsamuccaya kārikāyadā mama pareṣāṃ ca bhayaṃ duḥkhaṃ ca na priyam|tadātmanaḥ ko viśeṣo yattaṃ
rakṣāmi netaram

duḥkhāntaṃ kartukāmena sukhāntaṃ gantumicchatā|śraddhāmūlaṃ dṛḍhīkṛtya bodhau kāryā matirdṛḍhā

(śikṣādaro) mahāyānādbodhisattvasya saṃbaraḥ|marmasthānānyato vidyādyenānāpattiko bhavet

ātmabhāvasya bhogānāṃ tryadhvavṛtteḥ śubhasya ca|utsargaḥ sarvasattvebhyastadrakṣā śuddhivardhanam

paribhogāya sattvānāmātmabhāvādi dīyate|arakṣite kuto bhogaḥ ki dattaṃ yanna bhujyate

tasmātsatvopabhogārthamātmabhāvādi pālayet|kalyāṇamitrānutsargāt sūtrāṇāṃ ca sadekṣaṇāt

tatrātmabhāve kā rakṣā yadanarthavivarjanam|kenaitallabhyate sarvaṃ niṣphalaspandavarjanāt

etatsidhyetsadā smṛtyā smṛtistīvrādarādbhavet|ādaraḥ śamamāhātmyaṃ jñātvātāpena jāyate

samāhito yathābhūtaṃ prajānātītyavadanmuniḥ|śamācca na caleccittaṃ bāhyaceṣṭā nivartanāt

sarvatrāpacalo mandamatisnigdhābhibhāṣaṇāt|āvarjayejjanaṃ bhavyamādeyaścāpi jāyate

anādeyaṃ tu taṃ lokaḥ paribhūya jināṃkuram|bhasmacchanno yathā vanhiḥ pacyeta narakādiṣu

ratnameghe jinenoktastena saṃkṣepasaṃvaraḥ|yenāprasādaḥ satvānāṃ tadyatnena vivarjayet

eṣā rakṣātmabhāvasya bhaiṣajyavasanādibhiḥ|ātmatṛṣṇopabhogāttu kliṣṭāpattiḥ prajāyate

sukṛtārambhiṇā bhāvyaṃ mātrajñena ca sarvataḥ|iti śikṣāpadādasya bhogarakṣā na duṣkarā

svārthavipāka vaitṛṣṇyācchubhaṃ saṃrakṣitaṃ bhavet|paścātāpaṃ na kurvīta na ca kṛtvā prakāśayet

lābhasatkārabhītaḥ syādunnatiṃ varjayetsadā|bodhisattvaḥ prasannaḥ syāddharme vimatimutsṛjet

śodhitasyātmabhāvasyabhogaḥ pathyo bhaviṣyati|samyaksiddhasya bhaktasya niṣkaṇasyeva dehinām

tṛṇacchanna yathā śasyaṃ rogaiḥ sīdati naidhate|buddhāṃkurastathā vṛddhiṃ kleśacchanno na gacchati

ātmabhāvasya kā śuddhiḥ pāpakleśaviśodhanam|saṃbuddhottayarthasāreṇa yatnabhāve tvapāyagaḥ

kṣameta śrutameṣeta saṃśrayeta vanaṃ tataḥ|sāmādhānāya yujyeta bhāvayedaśubhādikam

bhogaśuddhiṃ ca jānīyātsamyagājīvaśodhanāt|śūnyatākaruṇāgarbheceṣṭitātpuṇyaśodhanam

grahītāraḥ subahavaḥ svalpaṃ ceda manena kim|na cātitṛptijanakaṃ vardhanīyamidaṃ tataḥ

ātmabhāvasya kā vṛddhirbalānālasyavardhanam|śūnyatākaruṇāgarbhāddānādbhogasya vardhanam

kṛtvādāveva yatnena vyavasāyāśayau dṛḍhau|karuṇāṃ ca puraskṛtya yateta śubhavṛddhaye

bhadracaryāvidhiḥ kāryo vandanādiḥ sahādarāt|śraddhādīnāṃ sadābhyāso maitrī buddhādyanusmṛtiḥ

sarvāvasthāsu satvārtho dharmadānaṃ nirāmiṣam|bodhicittaṃ ca puṇyasya vṛddhihetuḥ samāsataḥ

siddhiḥ samyakprahāṇānāmapramādāviyojanāt|samṛtyātha saṃprajanyena yoniśaścintanena ca
